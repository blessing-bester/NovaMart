NovaMart has a rich history of innovative marketing campaigns:

- 1995: "NovaPoints Loyalty Launch" — first rewards program campaign
- 2005: "Spring Savings Festival" — multi-city event promoting new seasonal products
- 2012: "EcoNova" — marketing push for sustainable product lines
- 2018: "Smart Shopper App" — mobile app launch with personalized offers
- 2021: "Holiday Happiness Campaign" — interactive online campaign with giveaways and discounts
- 2023: "AI Recommendations" — email marketing driven by AI-generated insights from customer behavior

Campaign records include budgets, key performance metrics, and lessons learned for future initiatives.
