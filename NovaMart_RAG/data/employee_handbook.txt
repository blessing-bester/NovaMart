NovaMart employees follow the Code of Conduct and HR policies:

- Working Hours: Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm; Saturday 10am to 4pm (store dependent)
- Leave Policy: 20 days annual leave, 10 days sick leave; additional leave per local labor laws
- Dress Code: Smart casual; uniforms provided for customer-facing staff
- Performance Reviews: Conducted twice per year
- Training: Mandatory onboarding program and annual safety and product training
- Communication: All internal memos via NovaMart portal; encourage collaboration and transparency
- Ethics: Zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or unethical business practices

The handbook also includes guidelines for emergency procedures, workplace safety, and conflict resolution.
