NovaMart allows customers to return products within 30 days of purchase, with proof of purchase. 
Products must be in original packaging. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. 
Items such as gift cards, personalized products, and clearance items cannot be returned. 

Extended Policies:
- Electronics and appliances must include all accessories and manuals for returns.
- Seasonal items (holiday decor, promotional merchandise) must be returned within 14 days.
- Bulk orders for business clients can be returned within 15 days, subject to inspection.

NovaMart continuously updates the policy to ensure customer satisfaction while protecting company interests.
