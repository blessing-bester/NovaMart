All products sold at NovaMart adhere to strict quality and safety standards.

- Electronics: 1-year manufacturer warranty; certified quality inspection required before stocking
- Home Essentials: Sourced from approved suppliers; seasonal stock rotation
- Groceries: Freshness guarantee; weekly quality checks for perishables
- Apparel: Compliance with ethical manufacturing standards; size labeling and tagging mandatory

Product Documentation:
- Each product category has a standard operating procedure (SOP)
- Inventory and restocking cycles documented in NovaMart ERP system
- Promotions and discounts must be pre-approved by store manager

NovaMart emphasizes sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging where possible.
