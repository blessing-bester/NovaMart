NovaMart store operations cover day-to-day procedures and logistics:

- Opening & Closing: Checklists for cash registers, stock inventory, safety checks
- Inventory Management: Daily reconciliation with ERP system
- Staff Scheduling: Rotas generated monthly and updated weekly
- Customer Service: Standard procedures for complaints, returns, and queries
- Safety Protocols: Fire drills, first-aid stations, and emergency contacts
- Delivery & Stocking: Guidelines for receiving, verifying, and shelving new stock
- Performance Tracking: Daily sales metrics recorded in ERP and dashboard reports

Operations manuals are updated quarterly to reflect new policies and regulations.
